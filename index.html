Why FixHaiti Matters

Across Haiti, broken roads, flooding streets, and damaged infrastructure create daily dangers that often go undocumented. Small failures in infrastructure can quickly become life-threatening problems during storms, floods, and nighttime travel. FixHaiti transforms public reporting into structured infrastructure intelligence. By mapping and tracking these failures, the platform makes problems visible, measurable, and impossible to ignore.
